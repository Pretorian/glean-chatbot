# Remote Work Policy

Employees may work remotely up to four days per week, subject to manager approval and team coverage requirements. All remote work must be performed from a location with secure internet access and an ergonomic workspace.

## Eligibility

- Employees must have completed their initial 90-day onboarding.
- Roles requiring physical presence (lab work, hardware, on-site support) are excluded.
- Managers may designate specific days as required in-office.

## Equipment

The company provides a laptop, external monitor, keyboard, and mouse. Home-office stipends of up to $500 per year cover ergonomic furniture or additional peripherals. Receipts must be submitted within 60 days.

## Security Expectations

- All work must be performed on company-issued devices.
- Public Wi-Fi networks require the use of the company VPN.
- Confidential conversations should occur in private, not in shared spaces or public venues.

## Time Zones and Availability

Employees should maintain overlap of at least four working hours with their primary team. Core collaboration hours are 10:00–15:00 in the team's primary time zone.
